WHO WE ARE

WE'RE A LOCAL TEAM OF CENTRAL FLORIDA PROFESSIONALS

Most of our staff are either from the Ocala area or have lived here long enough that it seems like a lifetime. We understand the business landscape here and have many connections within the local business community.

WHAT SEPARATES US FROM OTHER AGENCIES?

THE TEAM

Each project we manage is touched by at least 3-4 members of our seasoned staff. Our social media accounts aren’t managed by kids after school. Quality campaigns need effective communication, custom graphics, and quality content that communicates the right message at the right time.

Our staff consists of individuals with a true passion. Each member has at least 10+ years of experience in their respective fields.

CAPABILITIES

Our 2,500 SF video production studio is equipped with a green screen and the industry’s best tools. There’s an additional 4,500 SF of office space, video editing booths, and equipment storage.

We manage campaigns across a variety of verticals—TV commercials, billboards, Google Ads, and Facebook. We are a true, full-stack marketing partner.

STABILITY

Jeff Dillon, Founder, comes from a background in news media and has been a member of the Marion County business community since 1985. His businesses have continued to grow and prosper for over 35 years.

Through years of economic ups and downs, we've remained a stable and growing force in the Florida media landscape.

TRANSPARENCY

Open communication and transparency are the first steps towards building trust. We give clients full access to the accounts we manage and have a live interactive analytics dashboard that allows 24-hr monitoring of everything from Google Ads to email marketing statistics.

CONSISTENCY

Quality marketing campaigns require putting time in month after month. This is why we work to prove our worth month after month and do not require long-term contracts.

TRUST

Client trust is the ultimate gauge for our success. We understand that trust isn’t built overnight, but if we put in our time, do what we say, and give our clients positive results, we become successful.
