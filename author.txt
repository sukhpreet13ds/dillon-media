Faith Dominie

Professional Bio

Faith Dominie is an Ocala-based marketing professional, content creator, and Assistant Director of Marketing at Dillon Media Group. Since joining the agency, she has helped create strategic content for clients across a range of industries. Her writing experience also includes ghostwriting for successful business-focused book projects, including titles that became #1 Amazon best-sellers with recognition across multiple categories.

Before moving deeper into marketing, Faith developed a strong background in hospitality and customer service. That shaped her people-first approach to communication, giving her a natural ability to listen closely and create messaging that feels approachable and authentic.

Today, her work has supported businesses ranging from local service providers and professional firms to ecommerce brands, community organizations, and specialized suppliers.
