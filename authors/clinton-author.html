PROFESSIONAL BIO

Clinton Grubbs is the Director of Marketing at Dillon Media Group, where he leads digital marketing strategy, brand development, campaign planning, and web design projects for businesses serving Ocala, Central Florida, and markets nationwide.

Since joining Dillon Media Group in 2020, Clinton has worked closely with clients to understand their goals and build strategies that help strengthen their presence. His background combines marketing leadership, business ownership, communications, media, and hospitality, giving him a well-rounded perspective on both customer experience and business growth.

Before joining Dillon Media Group, Clinton owned a digital solutions company focused on helping small businesses generate more sales through custom websites and online marketing. His earlier experience also includes communications and media work in the petrochemical industry, as well as more than 15 years in hospitality, where he gained a strong sense for detail, service, operations, and customer relationships. That background brings depth to how he helps clients move toward their goals today.
